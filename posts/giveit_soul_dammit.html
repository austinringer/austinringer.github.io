Fuck Laziness

In my life I’ve met a handful of people that seem to operate on a completely different level than everyone around them. Those who find the time to score higher on tests, create better quality work, take care of their body and still date hotter women. Like Michael Jordan’s of everyday life they make it look easy. This pissed me off, until recently.

As a personal development exercise I made a point to befriend these high performing mutants and spend time absorbing their energy and techniques. The common denominator is this- laziness disgusts people that operate at a high level.

Gain Momentum

I recently read a book called Flow which basically vouches for the science behind being “in the groove” and minimizing distractions while doing really well at something. The author presents years of research to suggest that gaining control over your inner self leads to unparalleled happiness.

My main takeaway is this: Laziness in any part of your life can prevent “flow” in all of your life. Put yourself in situations that facilitate “flow” and experience the momentum that leads to growth as a human being.

Be Better!

If something is bothering you right now chances are you will be much better off if you address it right now. I am trying to make this a habit in my life and I like what I see so far.
