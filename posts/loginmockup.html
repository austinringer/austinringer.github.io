A Login Mockup For Work

This is a quick weekend design for an app my work teammates are building. The overlapping mountains concept is totally ripped from a Dribbble upload I found (link). I recreated the effect and added some UI elements in Sketch, my first time playing around with this app.

I'm realizing that I learn a lot faster when I reverse engineer projects from other, much better, designers. I'd go so far as to say that most of the time it is way more efficient to find a few designs I like, figure out how they were made, and finally put my own spin on it all (see the Venn Diagram below). This is probably just a side effect of my being a beginner, though, I still use this "head first" learning strategy with programming most of the time.


I'm always hugely grateful to the selfless folks on Dribbble, Behance, etc that share projects for free so here are my .sketch and .ai poject files just in case someone, somewhere has a use for them.
