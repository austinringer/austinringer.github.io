Constant Conversations

I'm grateful to have a handful of friends that are much, much smarter than I will ever be. I'm not self deprecating here, that's just a fact as I see it. This doesn't mean I don't have my moments. Deep middle of the night, alone to my thoughts I sometimes stumble face forward in glorious revery upon universal truths. Though, more often than not, I battle with the basics. My smart friends do a few things I find interesting. I'm going to take some time here to write about one of those things, unguided conversation.

Never thought of it that way

Without exception, every bright person I know sets aside time regularly to surround his or herself with a few mates for a chat about anything (and everything). It's a nameless ritual where a small group meets with a single understanding- let's talk.

It's never about "catching up" or having an excuse to drink to excess. No, it's about listening, riffing off one another to arrive at ideas that are impossible to reach by one's self. It's a step back from day-to-day life to talk about the abstract or zoom in on a microinteraction to examine it for deeper meaning. It's meditation... but less boring.

When I notice something I don't understand I like to tuck it away in my brain for later. This is was one of those things, until recently.

It's totally a thing


Three months ago I moved Florida --> California and lucked out finding a roommate with plenty of shared interests. I've quickly realized he's a bright guy. Good school, great job in tech, you know, all that stuff. He graciously invited me to dinner with a couple of his college friends a few weeks ago. As a stranger in a strange land I jumped on the opportunity. Seconds in, I realized what this was and the similarities astounded me. This was three smart people +me on a Friday afternoon talking about anything (and everything).

Skip to here if you don't like reading

This experience we each lead is meaningless without others. I'm probably a little late to the game here but I now consciously seek out opportunities to talk, just talk, about anything (and everything).
